Every decision has a cost. There is always a tradeoff. Time, money, labor and energy are all forms of currency in our economy. It's up to the individual what they are willing to sacrifice.

But not everything has such a profound impact on our lives. Some decisions are small and not so significant. Stopping by Tim Hortons every other morning for a double-double is a marginal choice. Others are life changing. It's important to weigh the pros and cons of each decision. What are you willing to give up? What are you willing to sacrifice for your own personal gain? This is the weight of tradeoff, opportunity costs. It is the value of the next best alternative that you give up when you make a decision.
